Alfredo Sauce

This classic Italian sauce is simple yet incredibly flavorful, featuring a creamy sauce made with Butter, cheese, cream, and parsley.

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat.
  3. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add cheese and garlic.
  5. Whisk continuously until heated through.
  6. Stir in parsley.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
